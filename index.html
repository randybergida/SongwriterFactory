Songwriting, Voice & Guitar Lessons in Greenpoint & Williamsburg, Brooklyn

Randy Bergida, founder of Songwriter Factory, has been professionally performing, producing, and teaching for the past 20+ years. He has spent his career performing at New York’s most coveted venues and bringing his music to stages across the country. His music has been featured in AM New York and CMJ, played on hundreds of radio stations across the country charting on FMQB, the winner of Airplay Directs All Things Digital, featured on MTV, iTunes favorite album, and CD Baby staff favorite.

Randy has recorded with industry heavyweights, including multi-Grammy award-winning producer Ken Lewis (John Legend, David Byrne, Beastie Boys), Joel Hamilton (Tom Waits, Dub Trio, Blackroc), and Tape Op legend Larry Crane (Elliott Smith, Stephen Malkmus). He was the lead singer, co-writer, lyricist and rhythm guitarist for the band Skidmore Fountain. He founded The Letter Yellow, a groovy, psychedelic rock experiment venturing into the land of 100% analog recordings, and New Indiana, an intimate duo made up of sheer vibe, cello, guitar and captivating vocal harmonies.

At Songwriter Factory, located in the heart of Greenpoint, Brooklyn, Randy provides one-on-one lessons tailored to each individual student. He translates his two decades of songwriting, studio and stage experience into a personalized curriculum. In addition to songwriting, guitar, and voice lessons, Randy offers specialized production and recording services at the studio. Songwriter Factory is equipped with handpicked, vintage-flavored, analog and digital recording equipment, designed to provide a rich musical atmosphere for refining your skills as a songwriter and musician. Holding a degree in composition and guitar from the University of Arizona, Randy’s teaching approach bridges theory and technique with his decades of songwriting, collaboration, recording studio, and stage experience.
