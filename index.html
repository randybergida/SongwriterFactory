Songwriter Factory was founded by Randy Bergida who has been professionally performing, teaching, writing, recording, and producing music for 20+ years. His music has been featured in AM New York and CMJ, played on hundreds of radio stations across the country charting on FMBQ, the winner of Airplay Directs All Things Digital, featured on MTV, iTunes favorite album, and CD Baby staff favorite. Randy has recorded with industry heavyweights, including Multi-Grammy award-winning producer Ken Lewis (John Legend, David Byrne, Beastie Boys), Joel Hamilton (Tom Waits, Dub Trio, Blackroc/The Black Keys), as well as Tape Op legend, Larry Crane (Elliot Smith, Stephen Malkmus). He was the lead singer, co-writer, lyricist and rhythm guitarist for the band Skidmore Fountain. He released Firebug under Randy Bergida in 2009. He founded The Letter Yellow in 2012 releasing his band's debut album ‘Walking Down the Streets’, in 2015 their 100% analog ‘Watercolor Overcast’, and in 2018, their 3rd record "On the Other Side". In 2018 he released the debut record "Darkness Sunshine" for New Indiana with longtime collaborator and friend Topu Lyo. He continues to write and release records prolifically with Brooklyn String Duo.

Randy regularly performs in New York’s most coveted music venues. Today, his focus is on mentoring the next generation of songwriters through Songwriting Lessons, Guitar Lessons, and Voice Lessons in Brooklyn (Williamsburg/Greenpoint).

In addition to teaching, Randy offers specialized Production and Recording Services at Songwriter Factory. The studio is equipped with handpicked, vintage-flavored, top-of-the-line analog and digital gear, designed to provide a rich musical atmosphere for refining your skills as a songwriter and vocalist. Randy holds a degree in composition and guitar from the University of Arizona.
