Songwriting, Voice & Guitar Lessons in Greenpoint, Brooklyn

Randy Bergida is a professional musician, producer, and educator with over 20 years of experience in the industry. Holding a degree in composition and guitar from the University of Arizona, Randy has spent his career performing in New York’s most coveted venues and recording with industry heavyweights. His credits include collaborations with multi-Grammy award-winning producer Ken Lewis (John Legend, David Byrne, Beastie Boys), Joel Hamilton (Tom Waits, Dub Trio, Blackroc), and Tape Op legend Larry Crane (Elliott Smith, Stephen Malkmus).

His work has been featured in AM New York and CMJ, played on hundreds of radio stations across the country, and featured on MTV and The Tonight Show. This professional rigor is the foundation of Songwriter Factory, located in the heart of Greenpoint, Brooklyn.

Today, Randy’s focus is on mentoring the next generation of songwriters through songwriting, guitar, and voice lessons in the Brooklyn area (specifically Williamsburg and Greenpoint). In addition to teaching, Randy offers specialized production and recording services at Songwriter Factory. The studio is equipped with handpicked, vintage-flavored, top-of-the-line analog and digital gear, designed to provide a rich musical atmosphere for refining your skills as a songwriter and vocalist.
