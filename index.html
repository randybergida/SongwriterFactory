Songwriting, Voice & Guitar Lessons in Greenpoint, Brooklyn

Randy Bergida, founder of Songwriter Factory, is a professional musician, producer, and educator with over 20 years of experience honing his craft alongside industry heavyweights. He has worked with multi-Grammy award-winning producer Ken Lewis (John Legend, David Byrne, Beastie Boys), Joel Hamilton (Tom Waits, Dub Trio, Blackroc), and Tape Op legend Larry Crane (Elliott Smith, Stephen Malkmus).

Randy has spent his career performing at New York’s most coveted venues and bringing his music to stages across the country. His music has been featured in AM New York and CMJ, played on hundreds of radio stations (charting on FMBQ), and recognized as a winner of Airplay Direct’s "All Things Digital." His work has also been featured on MTV and The Tonight Show, named an iTunes "Favorite Album," and selected as a CD Baby "Staff Favorite."

At Songwriter Factory, located in the heart of Greenpoint, Brooklyn, Randy shares this experience with his students. Today, he focuses on mentoring the next generation of songwriters through songwriting, guitar, and voice lessons in the Brooklyn area (specifically Greenpoint and Williamsburg). In addition to teaching, Randy offers specialized production and recording services at the studio. Songwriter Factory is equipped with handpicked, vintage-flavored, analog and digital gear, designed to provide a rich musical atmosphere for refining your skills as a songwriter and musician. Randy holds a degree in composition and guitar from the University of Arizona.
