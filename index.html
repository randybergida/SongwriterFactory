Songwriter Factory was founded by Randy Bergida who has been professionally performing, teaching, training, and touring for 15+ years. His music has been featured in AM New York and CMJ, played on hundreds of radio stations across the country charting on FMBQ, the winner of Airplay Directs All Things Digital, featured on MTV, iTunes favorite album, and CD Baby staff favorite. Randy has recorded with industry heavyweights, including Multi-Grammy award-winning producer Ken Lewis (John Legend, David Byrne, Beastie Boys), Joel Hamilton, Joel Hamilton (Tom Waits, Dub Trio, Blackroc/The Black Keys), as well as Tape Op legend, Larry Crane (Elliot Smith, Stephen Malkmus). He was the lead singer, co-writer, lyricist and rhythm guitarist for the band Skidmore Fountain. He founded The Letter Yellow in 2012 releasing his band's debut album ‘Walking Down the Streets’, in 2015 their 100% analog ‘Watercolor Overcast’, and in 2018, their 3rd record "On the Other Side". In 2018 he released the debut record "Darkness Sunshine" for New Indiana with longtime collaborator and friend Topu Lyo. You can find him regularly performing in NYC’s most coveted music venues. Along with providing Songwriting Lessons, Guitar Lessons and Voice Lessons in Brooklyn (Williamsburg/Greenpoint), Randy offers recording and production services. Songwriter Factory is equipped with handpicked and vintage flavored top of the line analog and digital recording equipement. He has built Songwriter Factory studio over the years to provide an ever growing musical atmosphere as the recording process is an essential element to refining ones musical skills as a songwriter and vocalist. Randy holds a degree in composition and guitar from the University of Arizona.
