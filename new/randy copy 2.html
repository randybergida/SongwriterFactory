Songwriter Factory was founded by Randy Bergida who has been professionally performing, teaching, training, and touring for 15 + years. His music has been featured in AM New York and CMJ, played on hundreds of radio stations across the country charting on FMBQ, the winner of Airplay Directs 2008 "All Things Digital", featured on MTV, and an "iTunes favorite album" as well as a CD Baby staff favorite. Randy has recorded with industry heavyweights, including Multi-Grammy award-winning producer Ken Lewis (John Legend, David Byrne, Beastie Boys), Joel Hamilton (Tom Waits, Dub Trio, Blackroc/The Black Keys), as well as Tape Op legend Larry Crane (Elliot Smith, Stephen Malkmus). He was the lead singer, co-writer, lyricist and rhythm guitarist for the band Skidmore Fountain and regularly performs at NYC's top music venues. His newest project, The Letter Yellow, has a new album due out June 2012. Along with providing Songwriting Lessons, Guitar Lessons and Voice Lessons in Brooklyn (Williamsburg/Greenpoint), Randy offers recording and production services. The Songwriter Factory project studio is equipped with handpicked and vintaged flavored top of the line analog and digital recording gear. He has built Songwriter Factory studio over the years to provide an ever growing musical atmosphere. After all, the recording process is an essential element to refining ones musical skills (especially for the songwriter and vocalist). Several students have taken their albums and ep's on tour throughout the U.S. and Europe with great success! Randy holds a degree in composition and guitar from the University of Arizona.
